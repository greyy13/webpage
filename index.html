Mission

Our mission is to foster a safe, harmonious, and empowered community through effective governance, proactive community engagement, and equitable access to essential services. We are committed to promoting social cohesion, environmental stewardship, and economic growth, guided by principles of transparency, accountability, and responsiveness to the needs of our residents.

Vision

To build a vibrant and inclusive barangay where every resident enjoys a high quality of life, where diversity is celebrated, and where sustainable development ensures a prosperous future for generations to come.


Values

1.Integrity: We uphold honesty, transparency, and ethical conduct in all our actions and decisions.
2.Community: We prioritize the welfare and interests of our residents, promoting inclusivity, diversity, and unity.
3.Service Excellence: We are dedicated to delivering high-quality, efficient, and responsive services to meet the needs of our community.
4.Accountability: We take responsibility for our actions and commitments, ensuring prudent use of resources and accountability to our constituents.
5.Collaboration: We foster partnerships and collaboration with stakeholders to achieve common goals and address community challenges.
6.Innovation: We embrace creativity, innovation, and continuous improvement to adapt to changing needs and enhance service delivery.
7.Environmental Stewardship: We are committed to promoting environmental sustainability and resilience, protecting and preserving our natural resources for future generations.
